More Presidential Commonalities

  • Both were elected to to the Presidency in '60, in two different centuries
  • Both have 7 letters in their last names
  • Both married in their 30's to women in their 20's
  • Both lost a son while in office
  • Both were assassinated while in office, and both on a Friday, in the presence of their wives
  • Both presidents had successors with the last name of Johnson
  • Both assassins were born in '08
  • Both assassins are most well known by 3 names
  • Ones assassin fled from a theater, and was apprehended in a warehouse
