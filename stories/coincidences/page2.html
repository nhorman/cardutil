Dual Deaths?


During the Continental Congress of 1775, these two individuals became close friends, eventually working together to draft the Declaration of Independence.  When one succeeded the  next as president however, their relationship frayed.  Reconciling in 1812, they started a correspondence that lasted 15 years.  On July 4, 1826, celebrating Independence day, one passed away at his Virginia estate, and the other passed hours later in Quincy, Massachusetts.  Which two famous presidents were they?
