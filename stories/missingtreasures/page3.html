The Great Missing Box

According to the Bible, God instructed Moses to create a gold covered wooden chest with a lid to house the two stone tablets of the Ten Commandments.  This box, known as the $ANSWER1, was carried by the Israelites in the desert for 40 years, after their exodus from Egypt, eventually finding a home in the Temple of Solomon.  Years later, during their war against the Philistines, the $ANSWER1 was lost.  While several claims have been made as to its current location, including Mount Nebo, Ethiopia, and Rome, France, and Ireland, its true location has never again been verified.
