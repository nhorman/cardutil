A Whole Section of A Building?



$ANSWER1, constructed in the Catherine Palace in the 18th century, near St. Petersburg, was a sight to behold.  Gold gilded mosaics, carvings and made of priceless materials, it was a sight to behold.  In 1941, the Palace was captured by Germany, and was disassembled for transport, after which.....it vanished.  Never to be seen again.  No records of its transport fate exist, and to this day has never been seen again.
