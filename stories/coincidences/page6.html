You end up where you start


Sometimes things come full circle.  This man was living on his wifes plantation in Manassas Junction, Virginia, when the US Civil War began.  On July 21, 1861, Confederate and Union troops met in the first major battle of the Civil War, known later as the battle of Bull Run, which ran directly through the plantation property.  Years later in 1865, when Robert E. Lee surrendered the confederate forces to Union troops, bringing an end to the war in the parlor of the same house where the war began.  Whos house was this?
